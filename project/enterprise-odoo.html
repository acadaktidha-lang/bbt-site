Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Odoo implementation take?
A focused configuration typically takes 8 to 12 weeks, while a fully customized enterprise ERP with integrations runs 12 to 24 weeks. We deliver in phases so you see value early instead of waiting for one big launch.
Can Odoo support multiple companies and currencies?
Yes. Odoo is built for multi-company, multi-currency environments with automated consolidated reporting across all entities.
What's the difference between Odoo Community and Enterprise Edition?
Community is the free, open-source core. Enterprise adds advanced features (accounting, studio, mobile apps), official support, and hosting options. We help you choose the right edition based on your needs and budget.
Can you migrate our existing ERP to Odoo?
Absolutely. We handle data extraction, cleansing, mapping, and validation from legacy ERPs, spreadsheets, and accounting systems, with parallel testing to ensure a safe cutover.
Is Odoo suitable for businesses in Pakistan and MENA?
Yes. We configure Odoo for local tax structures, multi-currency operations, Arabic/RTL support, and regional payment and logistics providers, all backed by deep Pakistan and MENA market knowledge.
What support do you provide after implementation?
We offer ongoing maintenance, cloud and server management, version upgrades, and continuous feature training through long-term support packages.
Can Odoo integrate with our existing software?
Yes. Odoo connects with payment gateways, logistics providers, e-commerce platforms, analytics tools, and accounting systems. When native integration isn't available, we build custom APIs to connect your stack.
