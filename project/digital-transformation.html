FAQ

Digital Transformation — Your Questions Answered

How much does digital transformation cost?
Assessment & Roadmap: $3,500–7,000. Transformation Program: $15,000–50,000+. Enterprise: custom quote. Cost depends on system count, data volume, and complexity. Free assessment comes first.
How long does digital transformation take?
Assessment: 2–3 weeks. Single system modernization: 6–10 weeks. Full business digitization: 3–6 months. Enterprise transformation: 6–12 months. Phased delivery means you see value every month.
Will we lose data during migration?
No. We use validated migration scripts, parallel running, and rollback plans. Every data point is verified before cutover. Zero-loss guarantee in our SLA.
Can you work with our existing systems?
Yes. We integrate with legacy ERPs, custom databases, and third-party tools. We build API layers and middleware when direct integration isn't possible.
Do we need to stop operations during transformation?
No. We use parallel running, phased cutover, and weekend deployments to ensure zero business disruption. Your team keeps working while we transform behind the scenes.
What cloud platform do you recommend?
We recommend based on your needs: AWS for flexibility and scale, Azure for Microsoft ecosystem integration, GCP for AI/ML workloads. Multi-cloud strategies available for resilience.
How do you measure transformation success?
We define KPIs upfront: process time reduction, cost savings, error rate reduction, user adoption, system uptime, and ROI. Monthly dashboards track every metric.
What happens after transformation is complete?
We provide 6-month post-launch support, optimization, and training. After that, monthly care plans or managed services. We don't disappear — we scale with you.
