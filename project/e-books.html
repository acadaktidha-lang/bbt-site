10. FAQ

Questions About Our E-Books & Guides

Are these guides really free? What's the catch?

Yes, 100% free. No credit card required. No hidden fees. We ask for your email so we can send you the PDF and occasionally share relevant insights. You can unsubscribe anytime. We create these guides because we believe valuable knowledge should be accessible, and because they demonstrate the depth of expertise we bring to every client engagement.

How often are the guides updated?

We review and update our guides quarterly, or immediately when major regulatory changes occur (like ZATCA Phase 2 updates or new FTA requirements). Each guide shows its "Last Updated" date, and subscribers receive notifications when significant revisions are published.

Can I share these guides with my team?

Absolutely. We encourage you to share them with colleagues, stakeholders, and partners. However, we ask that you don't republish them on external websites or claim authorship. If you'd like to reference our work publicly, please link back to the original download page.

Do you offer guides in Arabic?

Several of our GCC-focused guides (particularly the Odoo Implementation Playbook and ZATCA Compliance Guide) include Arabic-language sections, bilingual interface design guidance, and Arabic terminology glossaries. We're expanding our Arabic content library based on reader demand, so let us know what you need.

What format are the guides in?

Most guides are PDFs optimized for screen reading and printing. Some include interactive elements like checklists, comparison matrices, and fillable worksheets. We also offer select guides in PowerPoint format for executive presentations. File sizes range from 2MB to 10MB.

How long does it take to read a guide?

Our guides are designed for a single focused session, typically 30 to 60 minutes. We use visual frameworks, bullet points, and scannable sections so busy professionals can extract value quickly. Each guide includes an executive summary for C-level readers who need the highlights in 5 minutes.

Can I request a guide on a specific topic?

Yes! We love reader suggestions. If there's a topic you'd like us to cover, whether it's a specific Odoo module, an AI use case for your industry, or a GCC compliance challenge, email us at resources@bigbinarytech.com. Popular requests get prioritized in our content calendar.

Do the guides recommend specific vendors or products?

We strive for objectivity. When we compare platforms (e.g., AWS vs Azure vs GCP), we provide balanced analysis based on real project experience. We're transparent about our partnerships, but our primary goal is to help you make the right decision for your business, even if that means choosing a platform we don't specialize in.

What's the difference between a guide and a case study?

Guides are instructional. They teach you how to do something (implement Odoo, migrate to cloud, automate a process). Case studies are proof. They show you what we actually did for a specific client, including challenges, solutions, and measurable results. Both are valuable, but serve different purposes. Browse case studies at our case studies page.

I downloaded a guide but didn't receive it. What should I do?

Check your spam/junk folder first, because sometimes our emails land there. If you still don't see it within 10 minutes, email us at resources@bigbinarytech.com with the guide title, and we'll resend it immediately. You can also try downloading again from the same page, since our system recognizes returning visitors.
