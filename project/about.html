Our Story

The Binary Evolution

Big Binary Tech started in September,2015 with one simple goal: bring powerful enterprise technology to businesses of every size, not just big corporations. What began as a small software team has grown into a full digital transformation partner, offering Odoo ERP implementation, AI automation, custom software development, and POS solutions to clients across the world.

For us, "Binary" means a choice, between doing things the old way, or doing them the smart way. Every business we work with is choosing to move forward. Our job is to make that move easy, reliable, and worth it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Expert Answers to Common Inquiries

How long does an Odoo ERP implementation typically take for a mid-size business?

Eight to sixteen weeks, depending on your modules, workflows, and data volume. We phase it into; discovery, customization, migration, testing, and training. So your business never stops running.

Can you integrate AI automation with our existing CRM, ERP, or POS systems?

Yes. We use APIs and middleware to connect our AI automation to your current CRM, ERP, or POS without rebuilding anything. Your existing tools get smarter, not replaced.

Which industries benefit most from your AI and Odoo ERP solutions?

Retail, manufacturing, healthcare, logistics, education, hospitality, eCommerce, and professional services. These sectors run on repetitive workflows and multi-department data, exactly where our AI automation and Odoo ERP cut costs and speed up growth.

What does your post-launch support and maintenance include?

24/7 monitoring, bug fixes, security updates, performance tuning, and quarterly reviews. We also upgrade your AI models, ERP modules, and workflows as your business evolves.

How do you protect our data during cloud migration and ERP integration?

End-to-end encryption, role-based access, secure APIs, and encrypted backups. We run parallel systems during transition and align with compliance standards in the USA, UK, and GCC.

What makes Big Binary Tech different from other software development companies?

We combine AI automation, Odoo ERP, and custom software development under one roof, so you get one partner who understands your full technology stack instead of three vendors who blame each other when things break. Our PSEB registration, Cisco partnership, and government affiliations give you enterprise credibility at better value than typical US or UK agency rates.

Do you work with clients in different time zones?

Yes. We serve clients across the USA, UK, and GCC with overlapping work hours, async updates, and dedicated project managers who ensure you never wait a full day for a response. Our software development company has delivered Odoo ERP implementations and AI automation projects for businesses in New York, London, Dubai, and Riyadh without missing a deadline

How do you price your projects, fixed cost or hourly?

We offer both. Fixed cost for well-defined Odoo ERP implementations and custom software projects with clear scopes. Hourly for ongoing AI automation, digital transformation consulting, and evolving workflows where flexibility matters. Every proposal includes a detailed breakdown so you know exactly what you are paying for before we start.
