FAQ

Common Questions About Our Work

Can I see a case study relevant to my industry?
Absolutely. Use the filter bar above to browse by industry, or contact us and we'll send you the most relevant case studies for your sector.
Do you share actual client names and data?
We share detailed results and metrics with client permission. Some case studies use anonymized names for confidentiality, but all numbers are real.
How do you measure success for each project?
We define KPIs upfront during discovery: revenue growth, cost reduction, time savings, error reduction, and user adoption. Every case study shows before and after metrics.
Can I speak to a past client as a reference?
Yes. We're happy to connect you with past clients for reference calls once we've discussed your project.
What if my project is smaller than the ones shown?
We work with businesses of all sizes. Many of our case studies started as small projects that grew. Contact us and we'll propose an approach that fits your scale.
How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by scope: simple websites take 2 to 4 weeks, POS systems take 4 to 8 weeks, ERP implementations take 8 to 16 weeks, and digital transformation takes 3 to 6 months. Every case study includes timeline details.
