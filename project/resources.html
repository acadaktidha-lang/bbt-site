10. FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about services, pricing, timelines, and support.

What services does Big Binary Tech offer?
We deliver AI automation, Odoo ERP implementation, custom software, cloud migration, POS solutions, and end-to-end digital transformation for businesses across the GCC, USA, and UK.
How is your pricing structured?
Pricing is tailored to scope. We offer fixed-price packages for well-defined projects and flexible retainer or time-and-materials models for ongoing work. Every engagement starts with a free consultation and a transparent estimate.
How long does a typical implementation take?
A focused Odoo configuration typically takes 4–8 weeks, while a customized enterprise ERP with integrations runs 8–16 weeks. We deliver in phases so you see value every month rather than waiting for one big launch.
How do you handle data migration?
We run structured, validated migrations with full backups and reconciliation checks. Wherever possible we use a zero-downtime approach, migrating in parallel and cutting over only once data integrity is confirmed.
Can you modernize our legacy systems?
Yes. Legacy modernization is a core specialty. We re-platform aging systems into cloud-native, API-driven architectures while preserving your business logic and minimizing disruption.
Which cloud platforms do you support?
We're platform-agnostic and work across AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud, recommending the right fit based on your workloads, compliance needs, and budget.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Absolutely. Every project includes post-launch support, and we offer monthly care plans for dedicated support, system updates, monitoring, and continuous optimization as your business grows.
Do you help with GCC compliance like ZATCA?
Yes. We have deep experience with regional compliance including ZATCA e-invoicing and VAT reporting in Saudi Arabia, and we configure your systems to stay compliant out of the box.
What outsourcing and staffing models do you offer?
We offer dedicated teams, staff augmentation, and managed project delivery. You can scale a remote technical team up or down based on your roadmap and budget.
What makes Big Binary Tech different?
An AI-first approach, real GCC + international delivery experience, and a focus on business outcomes over technology buzzwords. We start with your process, then build the right solution around it.
Which industries do you specialize in?
Retail, manufacturing, healthcare, finance, real estate, government, professional services, and e-commerce, among others. Every engagement begins with industry-specific discovery.
How do I get started?
Book a free consultation through our contact page. We'll review your goals, recommend an approach, and provide a clear, no-obligation proposal.
