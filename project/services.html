07. FAQ

Common Questions About Our Services

Which services should I start with?
We start with a discovery session to understand your goals before recommending anything. Most clients begin with the service that solves their most urgent bottleneck, often AI automation or Odoo ERP, then expand from there as part of a connected roadmap.
How long does a typical project take?
A focused Odoo configuration or website typically takes 4 to 8 weeks, while a customized enterprise system with integrations runs 8 to 16 weeks. We deliver in phases so you see value every month rather than waiting for one big launch.
Can you integrate with our existing systems?
Absolutely. We integrate with ERP systems, accounting software, payment gateways, CRMs, and industry-specific tools. When native integration isn't available, we build custom APIs to connect your stack.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Yes. Every project includes post-launch support, and we offer monthly care plans for dedicated support, system updates, and continuous optimization as your business grows.
What makes your POS solutions different?
Our POS systems are built for multi-branch operations with real-time inventory sync, billing, and reporting in a single live view. They're customizable for both retail and restaurant workflows rather than forced into a generic template.
How does AI automation reduce costs?
By automating repetitive workflows, AI assistants, and integrations, your team reclaims hours spent on manual back-office work. Clients commonly see manual operations reduced by up to 70%, lowering operating costs and error rates.
