10. Candidate FAQs

Common Questions

Is this a remote job or do I need to come to an office?
All roles are remote-first. You can work from anywhere in Pakistan. We have optional co-working spaces in Lahore, Islamabad, and Karachi if you prefer an office environment. Team meetups happen quarterly.
What is the salary range for my role?
Salary ranges are listed on each job posting. We pay above market for every role. Final offer depends on your experience, skills, and interview performance. We're transparent about compensation from the first screening call.
Do you hire fresh graduates?
Yes, for select roles. We have a 6-month internship-to-hire program for developers and designers. You'll work on real projects with mentorship from senior team members. Apply via the "Intern" option in the Desired Role dropdown.
What technologies will I work with?
It depends on the role and project. Our stack includes React, Node.js, Python, Odoo, AWS, Azure, TensorFlow, OpenAI API, and more. We match you to projects that align with your skills and growth goals.
How do you handle time zones with GCC/USA/UK clients?
We have flexible hours. Core collaboration time is 2 PM - 6 PM PKT for GCC overlap. For UK/US projects, we adjust schedules or assign team members who prefer those hours. We don't expect anyone to work nights regularly.
What does the technical assessment involve?
A practical task relevant to the role, not algorithm puzzles. For developers: build a small feature. For designers: redesign a screen. For marketers: write a campaign brief. We give you 48 to 72 hours. It's designed to reflect real work, not interview theater.
How often do you promote?
We do quarterly performance reviews. Promotions happen when you're ready, not on a fixed schedule. 90% of our senior roles are filled internally. We publish clear skill matrices so you know exactly what it takes to level up.
I don't see my role listed. Can I still apply?
Absolutely. Use the "Other" option in the Desired Role dropdown. Tell us what you do and why you'd be a great fit. We hire proactively and often create roles for exceptional candidates.
