FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does social media marketing cost?
Our packages start at $800/month for the Starter plan and $1,800/month for the most popular Growth plan, with custom Enterprise pricing for full ad management, LinkedIn B2B, and Arabic campaigns. Ad spend is separate and always transparent. Book a free audit and we'll recommend the right fit for your goals.
How long before I see results?
Organic engagement typically improves within the first 4 to 6 weeks as we publish consistent, on-strategy content. Paid lead generation can produce qualified leads within the first month, and we report on performance monthly so you always know what's working.
Which platforms should I use?
It depends on your audience. We work across Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, Snapchat, and X/Twitter, plus Snapchat MENA and TikTok Arabia for GCC markets. During your free audit we identify the platforms where your customers actually are so budget isn't wasted.
Can you create Arabic content?
Yes. We create Arabic-first campaigns with RTL design, culturally relevant messaging, and native copywriting rather than literal translations. With 60% of GCC users preferring Arabic content, this is core to how we win local audiences.
How is ad spend handled?
Ad spend is billed directly to your own ad accounts and kept separate from our management fee, so you retain full ownership and transparency. We set clear ROI targets, track conversions, and optimize budget allocation across campaigns every month.
How do I track results?
You get real-time analytics dashboards showing campaign reach, engagement rate, leads generated, and cost per lead, plus a monthly report with actionable insights, not just numbers. You'll always know which content and campaigns drive ROI.
Can I pause or cancel?
Yes. Our plans are flexible. You can scale up, pause, or cancel with notice. There are no long-term lock-ins designed to trap you. We'd rather keep you because the results speak for themselves.
Do you work outside the GCC?
Absolutely. While we have deep GCC and Arabic expertise, we manage campaigns for clients across the USA and UK as well, covering English-language strategy, LinkedIn B2B lead generation, and paid advertising in those markets.
