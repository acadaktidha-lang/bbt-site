Industry Support

Industry FAQ

Do you have experience in my specific industry?
Yes. We've delivered solutions across 15+ industries including retail, manufacturing, healthcare, education, real estate, financial services, hospitality, logistics, construction, and government. Every project starts with deep industry discovery.
How do you ensure compliance with industry regulations?
Compliance is built into the foundation of every solution. We research your industry's regulatory requirements during discovery (like ZATCA, HIPAA, or GDPR), build compliance features into the architecture, and validate before go-live.
Can you integrate with industry-specific tools we already use?
Absolutely. We integrate with industry-standard platforms — medical devices, ERP systems, accounting software, payment gateways, logistics APIs, government portals, and more. Custom API development is available when native integration isn't possible.
How long does an industry solution take to implement?
Simple industry configurations take 4–6 weeks. Custom industry platforms take 8–16 weeks. Enterprise multi-system integrations take 4–6 months. We deliver in phases so you see value every month.
Do you offer Arabic support for GCC industries?
Yes — full Arabic RTL interfaces, Arabic documentation, bilingual training, and cultural adaptation. We test with native Arabic speakers to ensure natural workflow language.
What if our industry has unique workflows not covered by standard software?
That's exactly why businesses choose BBT. We don't force your workflows into generic software. We build custom modules, workflows, and interfaces that match how your industry actually operates.
How do you handle data migration from our old industry system?
We use validated migration scripts, parallel running, and data verification at every step. We offer a zero data loss guarantee and clean/structure data during migration for better performance.
What support do you offer after launch?
30-day post-launch support is included. After that, monthly industry care plans provide dedicated support, system updates, compliance updates, and continuous optimization. We grow with your industry.
